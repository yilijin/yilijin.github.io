About me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at McGill University under the expert guidance of Professor Xue (Steve) Liu. Previously, I obtained my M.Phil. degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in 2023 and my B.Eng. degree from Sun Yat-Sen University in 2021.

My research vision is to create a virtual world that people can enjoy. Towards this vision, my recent research interests include:

  • Multimedia Systems

  • Immersive Video

  • Telepresence
